Meet the barber

The guy behind the chair

So this whole thing is basically Yury's shop. He's been cutting hair around Bellmore for over two decades, and honestly a lot of his customers have been coming to him since they were little kids. Some of them literally grew up in that chair.

Him and Eddie run the place, and they actually know what they're doing — fades, classic cuts, beard stuff, all of it. People say it feels like getting a cut from family, not some random place that rushes you out the door. You come in, you talk, you laugh, you leave sharp.

The shop got fully renovated too, so it's clean and modern inside now — new chairs, fresh tile, the whole deal. But it's still the same friendly barbershop everyone knows.

— Yury & Eddie, Barber Crew
