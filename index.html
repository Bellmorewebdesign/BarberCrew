Meet the barber

About Yury and Eddie

This is Yury's barber shop. He has been cutting hair in Bellmore for over 20 years. A lot of his customers have been coming to him since they were little kids, and now they are grown up and they still come here.

Yury and Eddie both work here and they are both really good barbers. They do fades, regular haircuts, and beards. A lot of people say it feels like getting a haircut from family, because they take their time and talk to you instead of rushing you out.

The shop got fully renovated, so it looks clean and new on the inside now. There are new chairs and new floors. But it is still the same friendly barber shop that everyone already knows.

What we do

Services and prices

These are our prices. A haircut here is $25, and it is worth it because the barbers actually know what they are doing. A cheap haircut is not worth it if you do not like how it comes out.

Men's Haircut $25

A regular haircut done the way you want it. We can use scissors or clippers, whatever you like.

Skin or Taper Fade $30

A clean fade that blends from the skin all the way up to the top. This is the cut we are known for.

Kids Cut $20

We are really good with kids, even the ones who are nervous or do not want to sit still.

Beard Trim and Line Up $15

We shape your beard and clean up the edges so it looks neat and even.

Razor Edge Up $12

Sharp razor lines around your hairline and the back of your neck so your cut looks fresh.

Cut and Hot Towel $35

A full haircut with a hot towel at the end. It feels really good and relaxing.

Senior Cut $20

A regular haircut with a discount for older customers who have been coming here for years.

The Full Service $45

A haircut, beard trim, line up, and hot towel all together. This is the whole thing in one.

Gift Certificates Any $

You can buy a gift certificate for any amount, like $25, $50, or $100. Just call or come in.

Prices can change a little depending on how long your hair is and what you want done. You can walk in anytime, or call (516) 785-4995.

Good for the whole family

Kids haircuts

Getting your kid a haircut can be hard sometimes, especially if they are scared of the clippers or they do not want to sit still. We have been doing this for a long time, so we are really good with kids.

You can see the before and after right here. A lot of the parents who come here used to come when they were kids too, and now they bring their own kids to us.

  • We are patient with little kids and first timers
  • The shop is clean and parents like it
  • We work fast so kids do not get bored or upset
  • Your kid will actually like their haircut
